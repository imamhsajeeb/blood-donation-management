Objective

The main objective of the Blood Bank Management System is to manage the details of Blood, Donor, Blood Group, Blood Bank, Stock. It manages all the information about Blood, Blood Cell, Stock, Blood. The system can handle the information of blood bags, donors, recipients, and transactions in a blood bank. It can assist the blood test results, the quality of blood, the availability of blood, and the distribution of blood. It can also provide online storage, updation, and retrieval facility. It can reduce paperwork and save time.
